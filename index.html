Expert Tips

Tips for Better Notes

Implement these strategies to maximise your note-taking effectiveness every session.

📌 Be Selective

Don't write everything. Focus on key concepts, definitions, and examples. Quality over quantity always wins.

🎨 Use Colour & Symbols

Highlight important points, use icons, and colour-code topics for visual organisation and easy recall.

⏱️ Review Regularly

Review notes within 24 hours, then weekly. Spaced repetition strengthens memory and understanding.

📚 Summarise

Create concise summaries at the end of each chapter or lecture to reinforce your learning.

❓ Ask Questions

Write questions in margins while taking notes. Test yourself later to identify knowledge gaps.

🔗 Make Connections

Link new information with previous knowledge. Understanding connections deepens comprehension.

Revision

Revision Strategies

  • Review notes within 24 hours.
  • Use color-coded summaries.
  • Teach the topic to someone else.
  • Practice retrieval regularly.
Tip
💡

Spaced repetition boosts retention dramatically.

Returning to your notes at increasing intervals — after 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, then 1 month — is the single most scientifically proven method to lock information into long-term memory.
